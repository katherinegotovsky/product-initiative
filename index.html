The Product Initiative

Canada's national product development incubator. By students, for students.

About Us

The Product Initiative pairs talented technical students from the University of Waterloo with Canada's best business students at Western. Their mission? To build something BIG.

Over the course of the summer, teams will work together to build and launch software products. Through bootcamps, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities, we subject the participants of our program to the highest level of professional and personal growth.

Have your summer plans been impacted by covid-19? Have extra time on your hands? Apply today.

Our Program

Jun 22 - Jun 28

Bootcamp

Learn what it takes to be a successful PM, designer, developer, or business analyst.

Jun 29 - July 12

Product Ideation and User Research

Ideate on problems with your team. Identify problem spaces you are passionate about. Come up with the idea for something big.

July 12

Product Pitch

Pitch your product idea to a network of industry mentors — designers, product managers, developers, and analysts.

Jul 13 - August 17

Product Development

Work in teams to build out a real minimum viable product — a side project you will have under your belt for the rest of your career.

August 17

Final Product Pitch

Showcase your work to industry mentors, program participants, and national organizers. It's time to shine!

Our Roles

Learn more about the roles we have available!

Product Managers

Guide the success of your product. Define product vision, set the strategy, roadmap, and feature definition for your product. Manage a team of designers, engineers, and analysts to bring your product to life.

Developers

Architect and build your product. Gain experience in full-stack development to bring your product from idea to reality.

Designers

Use for eye for design to build a beautiful, user-friendly product. Craft journey maps, wireframes, prototypes and designs that create a high-quality experience for the product's end user.

Business Analysts

Develop a sustainable business model for your product by leading the creation of marketing & financial strategies. Build compelling pitch decks that sell others on your product.
