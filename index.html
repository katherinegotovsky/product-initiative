The Product Initiative.

Canada's national product development incubator. By students, for students.

About Us

We are a product incubator run for students, by students. Over the course of the year, four multi disciplinary teams of eight students from Commerce, Computer Science and Engineering compete to build and launch the best software product.

    Our Program

    Jun 22 - Jun 28

    Bootcamp

    Participants go through a bootcamp where they learn essential skills for their role.

    Jun 29 - July 12

    Product Ideation and User Research

    Team members meet each other. They meet and talk about problem spaces they want to tackle. They come up with a preliminary idea.

    July 12

    Product Pitch

    The teams pitch their product ideas to other program participants and mentors.

    Jul 13 - August 17

    Product Development

    The teams create their products.

August 17

Final Product Pitch

The teams pitch their MVPs to one another.
