The Product Initiative

Canada's national product development incubator. By students, for students.

About Us

The Product Initiative pairs talented student software engineers, designers, product managers and business analysts. Their mission? To build something BIG.

Over the course of our 2-month program, teams of students work together to build and launch software products. Their learning is supplemented through bootcamps, mentorship, and educational content - setting them up for professional and personal growth.

Since launching in May 2020, the program has grown to serve students at eight universities. Learn more about our products and teams below.

Team Structure

We recognize the transformative power of multidisciplinary collaboration and complementary skillsets. That's why each of our teams is made up of one product manager, one designer, one business analyst, and 2-3 developers.

Product Managers

Our product managers define their product's vision, strategy, roadmap, and features. Manage a team of designers, engineers, and analysts to bring your product to life.

Developers

Architect and build your product. Gain experience in full-stack development to bring your product from idea to reality.

Designers

Use your eye for design to build a beautiful, user-friendly product. Craft journey maps, wireframes, prototypes and designs that create a high-quality experience for the product's end user.

Business Analysts

Develop a sustainable business model for your product by leading the creation of marketing & financial strategies. Build compelling pitch decks that sell others on your product.
